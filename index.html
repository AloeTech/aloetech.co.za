A business plan is a written document that outlines the goals, strategies, and tactics of a business. It serves as a roadmap for entrepreneurs and business owners to guide their decision-making and ensure that their company is on the right track. A well-crafted business plan typically includes information on the company's mission and vision, target market, products or services, marketing and sales strategies, financial projections, and management structure.

It can be used for a variety of purposes, such as attracting investors, securing loans, or simply as a blueprint for the business owner to follow as they grow and expand their company. A good business plan should be clear, concise, and comprehensive, and should reflect a deep understanding of the industry, competition, and market trends.
